The Problem

Great product. No system behind the sales.

A two-person SaaS startup with a product that was genuinely solving a real problem — but growth was inconsistent. The founder was handling sales personally, reaching out manually on LinkedIn, following up when they remembered, and losing track of leads across a messy mix of email threads and a half-built spreadsheet.

When the founder was heads-down building, sales stalled. When they focused on sales, product slowed down. There was no middle ground because everything was manual and founder-dependent.

The Core Gap

The product had product-market fit. The bottleneck wasn't the product — it was that the sales process hadn't been built yet. Without a system, every new customer required the founder's full personal attention from first touch to close. That doesn't scale past 10 customers.

Demos were being booked with unqualified leads — people who weren't the right fit — because there was no qualification step before the call. The founder was spending 45 minutes on demos that should have been filtered out in 2 minutes.

The Solution

A founder-led sales system that runs on autopilot

01

Automated Cold Outreach Sequences

Targeted outreach goes to the ideal customer profile on a consistent weekly schedule — personalized emails, LinkedIn connection messages, and follow-ups — without the founder sending a single message manually.

02

AI Lead Qualification Before the Demo

When a lead shows interest, an automated sequence asks qualifying questions — company size, current solution, pain point, budget range — before a demo is booked. Only leads that meet the criteria get access to the founder's calendar.

03

Post-Demo Follow-Up Automation

After every demo, a structured follow-up sequence fires automatically — recap email same day, value-add content Day 3, objection-handling message Day 7, final check-in Day 14. The founder only steps back in when the lead responds.

04

CRM Pipeline with Stage Automation

Every lead is auto-logged and moves through pipeline stages based on their actions — opened email, booked demo, attended demo, went quiet. The founder sees a real-time view of exactly where every deal stands.

05

Trial-to-Paid Conversion Sequence

For SaaS products with a free trial, an automated onboarding and conversion sequence nudges trial users toward the paid plan — usage tips, feature highlights, and a time-sensitive upgrade prompt.

How It Gets Built

Live in one week. Running forever after.

Day 1–2

ICP & Funnel Audit

Define the ideal customer profile, map the current sales funnel, identify drop-off points, and set qualification criteria for demo access.

Day 3–4

Build the Pipeline

Set up outreach sequences, qualification flow, CRM stages, post-demo automations, and trial conversion sequences. Write all copy in the founder's voice.

Day 5

QA & Refine

Test every trigger, sequence, and CRM update end-to-end. Confirm qualification logic filters correctly and no leads fall through gaps.

Day 6–7

Launch & Handover

System goes live. I monitor the first week, make adjustments based on early data, and hand over a simple weekly reporting view.
