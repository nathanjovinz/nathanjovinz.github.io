The Problem

The investor was good at closing. Terrible at staying consistent.

A solo real estate investor with a solid track record — but deal flow was feast or famine. When actively prospecting, opportunities came in. The moment they got busy closing a deal, outreach stopped, the pipeline dried up, and they'd be starting from zero again in 60 days.

Follow-up was the biggest leak. Leads would show interest, go quiet, and by the time the investor circled back — sometimes weeks later — they'd already moved on. There was no system keeping those conversations alive.

The Core Gap

The investor's revenue was directly tied to how much manual effort they put in that week. A great system means deals move whether you're actively working them or not. Without it, every vacation, busy period, or closed deal costs you future pipeline.

There was also no visibility into what was working. Outreach happened through a mix of text messages, emails, and calls — nothing tracked, nothing measured, no way to know which leads were warm and which were dead.

The Solution

An automated deal flow pipeline that works while you close

01

Automated Outreach Sequences

Targeted outreach goes out on a consistent schedule — to motivated sellers, property owners, or off-market leads — without the investor manually sending a single message.

02

Lead Response & Qualification Bot

When a lead responds, an AI-powered system handles the first conversation — qualifying intent, gathering property details, and scheduling a call — before the investor ever gets involved.

03

Multi-Touch Follow-Up System

Leads that don't respond immediately enter a structured follow-up sequence — Day 3, Day 7, Day 14, Day 30. Each message is different in tone and angle. The sequence stops the moment a lead re-engages.

04

CRM Pipeline with Deal Stages

Every lead is automatically logged, tagged by stage, and moved through the pipeline based on their actions. The investor sees exactly where every deal stands without manually updating anything.

05

Dead Lead Reactivation Campaign

Leads that went cold 60–90 days ago get a reactivation sequence every quarter. Circumstances change — a seller who wasn't ready 3 months ago might be motivated today.

How It Gets Built

Fully operational in one week

Day 1–2

Audit & Strategy

Review current outreach channels, lead sources, follow-up gaps, and CRM setup. Define the ideal lead profile and deal criteria to target.

Day 3–4

Build the System

Set up outreach sequences, qualification bot, CRM pipeline stages, and follow-up automations. Write all message copy tailored to the investor's market and voice.

Day 5

Test Everything

Run every sequence end-to-end. Confirm triggers, responses, and CRM logging all fire correctly before going live.

Day 6–7

Launch & Monitor

System goes live. I monitor the first week of activity, fix any issues, and hand over a simple dashboard the investor checks weekly.
