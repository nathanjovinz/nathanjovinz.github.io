The Problem

The clinic was busy. The pipeline was leaking.

A dental clinic with one front desk staff member handling everything — calls, walk-ins, bookings, follow-ups, and reminders. Inquiries came in through Instagram DMs, the website contact form, and phone calls. Most got a response within a few hours, some the next day.

By the time they replied, the potential patient had already booked somewhere else. The clinic had no idea how many leads they were losing because there was no system tracking it.

The Core Gap

Speed of response was the difference between booking and losing a patient — not quality of care, not price, not location. Just response time. And that was entirely fixable with the right system in place.

On top of that, no-show rates were running at roughly 30–40% because reminders were sent manually — when the front desk had time — and not consistently. Each no-show was a direct revenue loss with no recovery mechanism.

The Solution

An automated patient pipeline that runs 24/7

Here's exactly what I would build — and in what order.

01

Instant Inquiry Response (Under 60 Seconds)

Every inquiry from the website form, Instagram DM, or Facebook page triggers an automated reply within 60 seconds — 24 hours a day. The message is warm, personal, and includes a direct booking link. No more leads going cold overnight.

02

Multi-Step Follow-Up Sequence

If the lead doesn't book within 24 hours, a follow-up sequence kicks in automatically — Day 1, Day 3, Day 7. Each message is different, non-pushy, and offers a direct path to book. The sequence stops the moment they book.

03

Appointment Reminder System

Every booked patient gets automated reminders — 48 hours before, 24 hours before, and 2 hours before their appointment. Each reminder includes a one-click confirmation and a reschedule link. No-show rates drop significantly with this alone.

04

Past Patient Reactivation

Patients who haven't visited in 3–6 months automatically receive a reactivation message. This recovers revenue from an existing base the clinic already paid to acquire — without spending a cent on new leads.

05

CRM Pipeline + Reporting Dashboard

Every lead, booking, no-show, and reactivation is tracked automatically. The clinic owner sees a live view of their pipeline without anyone manually updating a spreadsheet.
